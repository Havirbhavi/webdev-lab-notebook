Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released in 2014 by Google. It appears when users lose internet connection in Chrome.

Players control a dinosaur that jumps over obstacles and avoids enemies. Many people describe the game as simple but addictive.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game was released in 2019. It is a funny puzzle game where the player controls a mischievous goose.

The goal is to move around the village, complete tasks, and cause small problems for the people nearby.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird was released in 2013. It is a simple arcade game where the player taps to keep the bird flying.

The challenge is to avoid pipes and survive as long as possible. The game became extremely popular because of its difficulty.
